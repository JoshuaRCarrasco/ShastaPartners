Founded in October, 2002, Shasta Partners is a boutique investment bank focused on providing merger & acquisition, restructuring and capital raising services to technology and technology-enabled emerging growth companies.


Founder, Greg Burkus brings broad deal experience and deep industry relationships cultivated over an extremely active 20-year investment banking career, including more than 15-years at Alex. Brown & Sons, Morgan Stanley & Co. and Drexel Burnham Lambert.


Since 1988, Greg Burkus has successfully closed over a hundred transactions, ranging in value from $5 million to $1 billion. Transaction expertise ranges from executing sell-side and buy-side M&A, divestitures and recapitalizations to exchange offers, restructurings and high yield debt offerings to IPO’s, follow-on equity offerings, PIPE's and private placements.
